About Me

Sara Kingston

Chief Seedling Officer

Every spring, I get the itch to grow plants. The problem is, I move too much to keep a garden. So, I started growing plants from seed and selling the seedlings to fellow gardeners. By the time I head north for the summer, my plants have found new homes, and my gardening itch is officially scratched.

I've moved more than 20 times in my life, wearing many hats along the way: dentist, military officer, and hospitality entrepreneur. But no matter where I've landed, I've always found ways to dig in the dirt, grow something green, and (most importantly) spend time outside.

These days, I roam between the north woods of Minnesota, the outskirts of Los Angeles, the beaches of Florida, and the suburbs of Minneapolis. Wherever I go, you'll find me outdoors, soaking up sunshine, and of course, growing plants. 🌷

At The Roaming Gnome, my goal is to get outside and appreciate nature, and to help others do the same.
