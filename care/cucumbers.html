Including 8" and Pickling. Crisp and refreshing cucumbers are great fresh or preserved as pickles.

Growing Basics

  • Cucumbers are annuals in Minnesota.
  • Sunlight: Full sun (6-8 hours daily).
  • Soil: Moist, well-drained soil.
  • Transplanting: Plant at the same depth as in the pot when soil reaches 65°; handle gently to avoid disturbing roots. If your seedling container has multiple plants, you can plant up to 2 seedlings right next to each other (cut all but 2 down to soil level to avoid disturbing the roots); otherwise, space them 8-10" apart.
  • Watering: Water frequently to keep the soil evenly moist.
  • Fertilization: Use a fertilizer high in potassium and phosphorus (5-10-10) every 1-2 weeks once flowering starts. Avoid over-fertilizing with nitrogen to ensure proper fruiting.

Other Tips

  • Mulch to retain moisture and reduce weeds.
  • Harvest frequently to keep plants producing.
  • Provide a trellis for support.
