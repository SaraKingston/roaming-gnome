Mint Family

This group includes mint, peppermint, lemon balm, and catnip. Vigorous herbs with refreshing or calming scents. Perfect for teas, desserts, herbal remedies, or pollinator gardens.

Growing Basics

  • All are hardy perennials in Minnesota.
  • Sunlight: Full sun to partial shade.
  • Soil: Moist, well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
  • Transplanting: Plant at the same depth as in the pot.
  • Watering: Keep soil consistently moist, especially in containers.
  • Fertilization: Fertilize every 4-6 weeks with a balanced fertilizer (10-10-10).

Other Tips

  • These plants spread aggressively; keep in pots or root-prune regularly.
  • Trim frequently to encourage bushiness and prevent flowering (except for pollinator use).
