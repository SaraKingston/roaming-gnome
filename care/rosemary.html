Rosemary

Woody herb with needle-like leaves and a bold, piney aroma.

☀️
Sunlight
Full sun, 6+ hrs
💧
Water
When top inch dry
🌱
Type
Tender perennial
🪴
Soil
Sandy, well-drained

Growing Basics

  • Rosemary is a tender perennial in Minnesota; bring indoors during winter.
  • Sunlight: Full sun (6+ hours daily).
  • Soil: Well-drained, sandy soil.
  • Transplanting: Plant at the same depth as in the pot.
  • Watering: Water when top inch of soil feels dry.
  • Fertilization: Fertilize lightly every 6-8 weeks with balanced fertilizer or compost.

Other Tips

  • Prefers drier conditions; avoid overwatering.
  • Prune often to keep plant bushy and usable indoors.
