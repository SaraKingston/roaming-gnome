Basil

Fragrant herb with bright green or purple-tinged leaves. Sweet basil is perfect for Italian dishes and pesto, while Thai basil adds a licorice note to Asian cuisine.

Full sun, 6+ hrs

Growing Basics

  • Basil is an annual in Minnesota.
  • Sunlight: Full sun (6+ hours daily).
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil.
  • Transplanting: Plant at the same depth as in the pot after danger of frost has passed and soil is at least 60°F.
  • Watering: Water regularly, allowing the top inch of soil to dry between waterings.
  • Fertilization: Fertilize every 4-6 weeks with a balanced fertilizer (10-10-10). Avoid over-fertilizing to maintain flavor.

Other Tips

  • Pinch off flower buds to promote leaf growth and bushiness.
  • Harvest often.
