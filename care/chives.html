Chives

Mild onion- or garlic-flavored green stems perfect for garnishing, salads, and spreads.

💧

Growing Basics

  • Chives are perennials in Minnesota.
  • Sunlight: Full sun (6+ hours daily).
  • Soil: Fertile, well-drained soil.
  • Transplanting: Plant at the same depth as in the pot.
  • Watering: Keep soil evenly moist, but not soggy.
  • Fertilization: Fertilize every 4-6 weeks with balanced fertilizer (10-10-10).

Other Tips

  • Snip leaves regularly to encourage new growth.
  • Divide clumps every few years to keep plants healthy and productive.
  • I recommend planting these in pots; they'll take over your yard if they go straight in the ground.
