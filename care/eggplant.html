Eggplant

A warm-season favorite with glossy skin and creamy flesh. Wonderful grilled, roasted, or in stir-fries.

☀️

Full sun, 6+ hrs
💧
Water

🌱

🪴

Growing Basics

  • Eggplants are annuals in Minnesota.
  • Sunlight: Full sun (6+ hours daily).
  • Soil: Loamy, well-drained soil, rich in organic matter.
  • Transplanting: Plant at the same depth as in the pot, avoid disturbing the roots.
  • Watering: Deep water weekly, keep soil consistently moist.
  • Fertilization: Use balanced fertilizer (10-10-10) every 2-3 weeks; switch to phosphorus-heavy fertilizer (5-10-10) when fruiting begins.
  • Stake to support heavy fruit, mulch for moisture.
